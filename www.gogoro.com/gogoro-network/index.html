Supporting 24/7 Urban Grid Stability

Did you know that Gogoro’s GoStation sites can also contribute to a Virtual Power Plant (VPP)?

Gogoro’s VPP is activated to ensure constant grid stability across urban area. When a city's grid nears capacity or is in an imbalanced state, GoStation sites can serve as distributed energy resources to dynamically balance and support grid stability.

Did you know?

Gogoro's VPP functions by swiftly responding to grid imbalances. When there's an irregularity that disturbs the grid's supply and demand equilibrium, GoStation sites will pause charging within seconds, mitigating the risk of blackouts.

With over 2,500 GoStation sites actively participating in the VPP, our GoStation network serves as a crucial energy resource for Taiwan Power Company’s Ancillary Service market. In times of grid imbalances, our GoStation sites offer vital flexibility to the grid across 1,000 locations in Taiwan, helping to relieve strain and ensure the stability of the city's electrical infrastructure.

Gogoro participates in the VPP only when circumstances permit, prioritizing the needs of subscribers first by ensuring our battery swapping experience remains uninterrupted.

As such, our subscribers can rest assured, continuing to enjoy the convenience and reliability of battery swapping, while simultaneously contributing to a greener and more sustainable future.
