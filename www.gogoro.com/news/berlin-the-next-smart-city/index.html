August 2, 2016

Berlin: The Next Smart City

Company, Partners, Smart Vehicles

We are pleased to announce we are partnering with Coup, the new smart e-scooter sharing service launching in Berlin today.

Coup, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Bosch, will start today with 200 Gogoro Smartscooters in the Berlin neighborhoods of Mitte, Prenzlauer Berg, Friedrichshain, and Kreuzberg. The service is available for a flat-rate fee: 3 euros for 30 minutes or 20 euros for the whole day. The service is accessed via the Coup app, making it simple to find, book, and pay for a scooter.

If you are in Germany and interested in joining Coup, check out www.joincoup.com.
