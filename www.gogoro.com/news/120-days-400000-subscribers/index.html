120 Days, 400,000 Subscribers

Company, Gogoro Network, Services
4,000 vehicles deployed, over 400,000 subscribers accumulated, this is the amazing result GoShare achieved in just 120 days after launch.

Being the first electric scooter sharing service that offers both light and heavy class scooters, and the only service that offers instant riding range extension via battery swapping, GoShare became the largest electric ride sharing service in Taiwan in just 4 months.
