September 16, 2021

Swap & Go: Bringing Battery Swapping to the World's Urban Riders

Company, Partners

The next chapter in Gogoro's mission to put smart portable electric power within reach of every urban rider worldwide begins today. We’re excited to announce that we've entered into a definitive merger agreement with Poema Global Holdings Corp. (NASDAQ: PPGH), a publicly traded special purpose acquisition company.

Since our founding in 2011, our innovative two-wheel electric vehicles and 360-degree platform, and cutting-edge battery swapping technology have made urban mobility more sustainable and efficient. We're thrilled to expand on these ambitions and bring EV riders across the globe access to reliable, safe and sustainable electric power as a public company.
