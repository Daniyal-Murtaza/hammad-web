Accelerating Electric Mobility in India Through Innovation and New Business Models: CEO’s from Gogoro and Hero MotoCorp Speak at BloombergNEF New Delhi Summit

Company, Gogoro Network, Smart Vehicles, Services, Partners

The BloombergNEF New Delhi Summit is a gathering of the leaders in the investment, policy, industrial and energy communities to discuss the key issues that impact the transition to a cleaner future in India. 

 

Our India-based joint-venture combines the power and reach of Hero Motorcorp with the innovation and battery swapping infrastructure experience of Gogoro to solve the complex challenges of electrifying India’s two-wheelers to usher in a new era of sustainable urban mobility in India – and beyond.

Dr. Pawan Munjal and Horace Luke headlined the final session at this year's virtual New Delhi summit. As two-wheel riders in India transition to electric, Gogoro's battery swapping tech/platform and services coupled with Hero MotoCorp's strong brand and manufacturing strength are poised to kickstart the impending urban mobility revolution.

 

“If we are going to transform ourselves from traditional modes of transportation to this new era of transportation I think new opportunities will surface and innovators are going to grab onto it and create new business models.” 

 

Horace talked about establishing standards that don’t interfere with new innovations and business models but maintain safe experiences. 

 

"I think standardization around safety and quality is really important but we also need to focus on innovation and flexibility for innovators such as Dr. Munjal and I to create new platforms and new vehicles and new solutions for the consumer.”
