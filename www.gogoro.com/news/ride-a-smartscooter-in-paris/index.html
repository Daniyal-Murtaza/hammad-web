August 16, 2017

Ride a Smartscooter in Paris

Company, Partners, Smart Vehicles

Today, we are excited to announce that Gogoro and COUP launched the COUP sharing service in Paris. 400 Gogoro Smartscooters are now available to rent throughout central Paris.

Announced in May, the COUP sharing service has received overwhelmingly positive press reviews and substantial preregistration sign-ups.

COUP’s availability in Paris follows its positive momentum in Berlin where it launched last summer with incredible success. COUP Berlin now has 1000 Gogoro Smartscooters on the streets of Berlin.

Learn more about how to ride COUP in Paris and sign up at https://joincoup.com/fr/paris/.
