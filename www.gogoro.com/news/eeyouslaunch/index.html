A New Kind of Ebike: Gogoro Launches Eeyo 1 in the US

Smart Vehicles, Company

In May, we unveiled our new ultralight Eeyo 1 E-Bike, and it launched today in the US at https://eeyo.bike.

We redesigned the ebike to be streamlined and simple, yet still powerful and ultralight. At just 26lbs, Eeyo 1s is half the weight of typical ebikes and designed to be quick, responsive, and more exciting to ride.

It is a new category defining ebike for people living in cities that are looking for a more agile and fun ride that also provides maximum portability and a low-to-no maintenance ownership experience.

It's got everything you need, and nothing you don’t.

Crafted without compromise from carbon fiber you’ll feel like you're riding a bike, not a battery.
