For Sustainability

Sustainability in action. With vehicle panels made from 100% recyclable polypropylene, and some components are even made from post-consumer recycled PP.

Recycling for Good

We believe that every individual can make a positive impact on the environment through small changes in their daily lives. Gogoro and MUJI have joined hands in the creation of the "Recycling for Good" project.

Collect, shred, granulate.

We also recycle the wasted PP materials generated during the vehicle manufacturing process, and transform them into reusable materials. This practice is a symbol of our brand's mission to create sustainable circularity.

The Recycling Process

Recycled, reused.

Through reshaping and remanufacturing the recycled items, we incorporate them into components that perfectly match the vehicle's design. It can be said that each vehicle is "co-created" by everyone who participated in the recycling process.
