Scooter key in Apple Wallet
Your iPhone and Apple Watch are now your scooter key.
With Express Mode, you don’t need to wake or unlock your iPhone or Apple Watch, or open Gogoro App, to use your scooter key in Apple Wallet.
A few simple steps let you share your scooter key with friends or family easily.
If your iPhone needs a charge, you can still get into your vehicle via Power Reserve mode.
You can add your key to both your iPhone and Apple Watch.
When your scooter key is in Apple Wallet on your iPhone or Apple Watch, they’re stored on your device, which means Apple doesn’t see when or where you unlock your vehicle. Your data is private and secure.
Open your Gogoro App on your iPhone. Find your scooter key, and tap Add to Apple Wallet. Then use your iPhone or Apple Watch for easy access to your scooter.
